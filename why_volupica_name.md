Volupica because:
- The suffix -ica is known in European languages.
- Volupica is easier to pronounce for east asians than Volupum or Volupicum.
- Compared with Volupa and Volupu: Volupica sounds:
    - more like a real word and less like an arbitrary word.
    - more like a name related to Volupism.
    - more like an institution and less like a product. 
- Volupica sounds like it is written unlike Volupe.
